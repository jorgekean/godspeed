# GodSpeed Grader - Teacher Workflow Guide

## How to Use GodSpeed Grader (Step by Step)

### 📋 Step 1: Create an Exam (In the App)

1. Open GodSpeed Grader on your phone or tablet
2. Go to **"Create Exam"** section
3. Fill in:
   - **Exam Name** (e.g., "Math Quiz - Chapter 5")
   - **Number of Questions** (Choose 20 items or 50 items)
   - **Subject** (e.g., Mathematics, Science, English)
4. Click **"Create"** — Your exam is now ready!

---

### 🖨️ Step 2: Download & Print Answer Sheets (Use a Laptop/PC)

> ⚠️ **Best Practice**: Use a laptop or desktop computer for this step. Mobile printing can be unreliable.

1. Open GodSpeed Grader on your phone or tablet
2. Click **"Download 20-Item Sheet"** and **"Download 50-Item Sheet"**
3. **Save the file** to your computer
4. **Open the PDF** on your laptop/desktop
5. Click **"Print"** and make sure:
   - ✅ Do NOT scale or fit to page
   - ✅ Print at 100% size
   - ✅ Use white paper (8.5" x 11" or A4)

---

### ✏️ Step 3: Students Answer the Sheet (Outside the App)

1. **Distribute the printed answer sheets** to students along with the exam questions
2. Students:
   - Read each question
   - Fill in their answer by **completely shading the bubble** (A, B, C, or D)
   - Use a **pencil** or **black pen** for best scanning results
3. Collect all completed sheets

---

### 📱 Step 4: Scan & Grade (Back in the App)

#### 4a. Open the Exam
1. Open GodSpeed Grader on your phone/tablet
2. Find and tap the exam you created earlier
3. Tap **"Start Scanning"**

#### 4b. Scan Each Answer Sheet
1. **Point your phone camera** at the completed answer sheet
2. Align all 4 corners of the sheet inside the **blue dotted box** on screen
3. When aligned, the app will:
   - 📸 Automatically capture the sheet
   - 🤖 Scan all the bubbles
   - ✅ Extract the student's answers
4. The app shows the results instantly:
   - Student score
   - Number of correct answers
   - Answer breakdown

#### 4c. Review Ambiguous Answers
- If the scanner isn't sure about an answer (the bubble was partially filled), you'll see:
  - ❓ **"Ambiguous Answer"** message
  - The question number
- Look at the **physical paper** and select the correct answer yourself
- The app then includes this in the final score

#### 4d. Move to Next Student
1. Click **"Scan Next Paper"**
2. Repeat steps 4a-4c for each student

---

## 💡 Pro Tips

✅ **Best Results:**
- Use good lighting when scanning
- Keep the sheet flat and straight in frame
- Use black bubbles (not light pencil marks)
- Make sure the entire sheet is visible

❌ **Avoid:**
- Scanning while moving
- Very dim lighting
- Light pencil marks
- Folded or wrinkled sheets

---

## 🔒 Privacy & Security

- All processing happens **on your phone only**
- **No data is uploaded** to the internet
- Your grades stay with you
- Perfect for offline use

## Need Help?

If something isn't working:
1. **Check lighting** — Make sure you have good light
2. **Check sheet alignment** — Align all 4 corners in the blue box
3. **Use fresh sheets** — Wrinkled or faded sheets may not scan well
4. **Try again** — Sometimes one or two attempts are needed
